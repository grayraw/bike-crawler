Rider Position / Bike Geometry

Pure Modern XC. Low flat back, centered weight distribution.

Innovation AND Technology

Clean Cable Management

Clean cable management:

Interchangeable in-head tube cable guides hold the housing securely and support every possible cable configuration.

INTEGRATED DI2 BATTERY HOUSING

Integrated Di2 battery housing:

Internal Top Tube Di2 battery holder. Easily accessible. Allows the use of stealth dropper post and Di2.

MODERN XC GEOMETRY

Modern XC Geometry

Short chainstays for traction and agility, slack head angle for stability at speed, tight 'Trail' measurement for nimble handling at slower speeds - perfect for modern XC.

Ultralight Carbon Link

Ultra light weight -

System Integrated cranks, fork, frame and Zero-Pivot stays all add up to a light-weight full suspension XC mountain bike.
